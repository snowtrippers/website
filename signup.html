How to Join SnowTrippers Google+ community

The community is private so a administrator needs to approve your membership.

#1. Sign up for google+

Note: You can use any gmail address.
I can also provide you with a SnowTrippers.com email address.

Note: Follow steps in #2 or #3

#2. add me to a circle

  1. sign into google+
  2. go to my google+ page
  3. you should see an "add to circles" button below your photo a top of page, click it. click it hard.

  4. wait for invite - when I see you've added me to a circle, I'll send you an invite to the community.

#3. request to join the community

  1. sign into google+
  2. go to the community
  3. Click the "Ask to Join" button
    .

    After clicking the "Ask to Join" button, the button should change to "Cancel Join Request". If not try adding me to a circle (see #1). Note, some people have had troubles with this not working.




I'll respond as quickly as I can to requests
